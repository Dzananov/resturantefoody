About Us


Welcome to Foody Hamburger Resturant! Foody was started in 2022 and is Based in Sweden, Malmö. We have two resturantes but hopefully we will be able to open more resturantes outside of Malmö. Foody has developed a special hamburgersauce that makes our hamburger taste unique. All our ingridiences are from Sweden and therefor ecological. We can assure you that you will be pleased with our food. Imagin eating a hamburger with freshly baked bread, perfectly grilled meat, melted cheese, fresch vegetables and than our magical sauce. You wont regrate visiting us!

Bakery

We bake our delicious bread at our own bakery. That way we can garantee that the bread is fresch when served. We get provided by Swedish farmers for our grain. In that way we can keep oyr bread ecological

Meat

Our cow farmers are all approved by the Swedish health department and the County-board. We are very keen on keeping healthy cows to produce the best meat. We only buy from farmers that keep their cows in a big facility with alot of outdoor time. We alos prefer that cows eat ecological food and hay.
