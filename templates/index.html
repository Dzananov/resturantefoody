About Us


Welcome to Foody Hamburger Resturant! Foody was started in 2022 and is Based in Sweden, Malmö. We have two resturantes but hopefully we will be able to open more resturantes outside of Malmö. Foody has developed a special hamburgersauce that makes our hamburger taste unique. All our ingridiences are from Sweden and therefor ecological. We can assure you that you will be pleased with our food. Imagin eating a hamburger with freshly baked bread, perfectly grilled meat, melted cheese, fresch vegetables and than our magical sauce. You wont regrate visiting us!

Bakery

We bake our delicious bread at our own bakery. That way we can garantee that the bread is fresch when served. We get provided by Swedish farmers for our grain. In that way we can keep oyr bread ecological. We also always use freschly baked bread and yesterdays bread we donate to whoever is in need for it! We have different kinds of bread, white bread, dark bread, grainless-bread, lactofriendly bread and bread with a base made of oats

Meat. ..or no Meat?

Thats the thing with Foody, our burgers are completle meatfree! We only serve plant based burgers made from finest soybeans and peas and with a little pinch of quionoa. All produced by Swedish farmers in Sweden.
